Campus Ambassador Program

About Aarohi :
Aarohi is the cultural festival of Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, Nagpur.
It was first organised by the pioneer batch of 1988. In it's journey of 30+ years, it has expanded to become the Central India's largest cultural festival. With new additions every year, Abhivyakti, A La Danse, Cynosure, Pentathlon, Purple Haze and swar manzar are the few signature competitions organized at every Aarohi. It has hosted popular music concerts of Papon as well Euphoria, The Local Train, Nalayak. Well known stand up comedians like Kanan Gill and Zakir Khan have also been a part of Aarohi's proshow legacy. Aarohi always aims at expanding and widening it's reach to a greater audience.
